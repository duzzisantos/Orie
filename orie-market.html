Markets in Aba

Ariaria

Ariaria is one of the biggest markets in Nigeria. Established in the 1970s, it generates nearly US$1,000,000 in daily GDP.

Details

Chairperson: Ryan Agulanna

Website: www.ariaria.aba

Office Address: 50 Nowhere street, Aba.

Ekeoha

Ekeoha is a big market in Aba's downtown area. This colonial era market deals with general merchandise, textiles and garments.

Details

Chairperson: Trisha Igbokwe

Website: www.ekeoha.aba

Office Address: 40 Nowhere street, Aba.

Ahia Ohuru

Ahia Ohuru, known in English as "New Market" is one of the newer markets, eventhough it was established in the early 1990s.

Details

Chairperson: Jeff Obi

Website: www.ahia-ohuru.aba

Office Address: 30 Nowhere street, Aba.

Cemetary Market

This market deals with wholesaling of provisions, medications, cosmetics and care products. Merhcandise is sold here too.

Details

Chairperson: Cassidy Orji

Website: www.cem-market.aba

Office Address: 20 Nowhere street, Aba.

Asannetu

This market specialises in automobile accessories, otherwise known as "spare parts". It serves as a regional market in this specialisation.

Details

Chairperson: Patrice Kalu
